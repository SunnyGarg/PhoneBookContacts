PhoneBook Contacts

Accessing the contacts information from PhoneBook is very very complex process for a developer. We have simplified this process for our next developers. Now a developer can get all contacts information in just 3 easy steps.

3 Steps Integration Process

Step 1 -> Add below permissions in manifest.xml uses-permission android:name="android.permission.READ_CONTACTS" uses-permission android:name="android.permission.WRITE_CONTACTS"
